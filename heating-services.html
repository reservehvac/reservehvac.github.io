Heating Services

When the chill of winter is at its peak, you want your home to be a cozy, comforting relief from the cold. Let us make sure your home is well heated during those cold winter days and if your heating system does go down, we can fix it at anytime.

Our Heating Services include:

Furnace Installation

Furnace Repair

Furnace Maintenance

Heating Unit Installation for Renovations or Construction

Choosing the right heating solution is an important and difficult decision. If you are doing renovations or construction, this choice should be a step in the initial planning process and a step that you should include your HVAC contractor in on! At SameDay Services, we would be honored to help.

Electric or Gas Furnace Replacement

Furnaces, which utilize ducts to distribute clean and heated air, are how many people choose to keep their homes warm and comfortable. Eventually your furnace may need to be replaced for a variety of reasons. We can check the AFUE or annual fuel utilization efficiency to measure your appliances energy efficiency, and help you to compare it to other heating systems in the marketplace. Upgrading to a new model has the potential to cut your bills down by half, but it is important to get a heating contractor to choose the right model and right size for your home.

Furnace Repair

Before you turn on your heat each year, SameDay Services suggests that you get your furnace cleaned and inspected. Common services include replacing filters, repairing a blown fuse, replacing worn parts and adjusting the furnace thermostat. By being proactive, you can avoid a possible emergency during the middle of winter.

Heat Pump Repair and Replacement

Heat pumps move heat rather than generate it, and they are often used in homes instead of furnaces or air conditioners when your heating needs are not extreme. Proper maintenance will extend the lifetime of a heat pump. Heat pumps over 10 years old should be looked at by a professional contractor and possibly replaced. If your heat pump has failed, call us at 904-352-8400 and we will get someone on the job as soon as possible.

Hybrid Heating System Installation

Do you want a hybrid heating system installed? A hybrid system combines two heating sources (a furnace with a heat pump) to save energy and deliver heat. Hybrid heating systems can also cool down your home in the hot months allowing you to save energy all year. Contact us to learn more about the hybrid heating system and how it automatically uses the best method to control the temperature in your home.

Radiator Repair or Replacement

If you live in an old home, you may have a boiler-radiator heating system that is on its last leg. Radiators are often in apartments or houses with multiple stories. If well maintained, your system may be fine and you just want to schedule a tune-up. If you would like to update your home, and get a forced air heating system in place, SameDay Services can do that as well.

Gas or Electric Water Heater Replacement

Is your water heater storage tank leaking or making loud noises? Is your water heater not producing hot water? Does the water have a bad odor? Is the water discolored? We suggest you get a contractor to check out these issues for any electrical issues or malfunctioning parts.

Tankless Water Heater Installation, Repair, Replacement

Tankless Water Heaters usually create hot water as needed rather than store it in a tank. There are different models, but are all seen as an energy friendly option. Tankless Water Heaters do not waste the energy by heating water and then storing it.

Thermostat Replacement

Is the thermostat on your air conditioning unit or furnace not working properly? The thermostat directs the equipment on when and how much air or heat to produce. The thermostat may need to be reset or replaced. Common reasons for malfunctions include the thermostat battery being bad or the components not being clean.

Duct Work

Ducts are sources of airflow, which deliver and remove air, and help keep residents comfortable in their homes. If the ducts were not properly installed or maintained, they will likely become dirty and eventually need to be serviced or replaced. It is difficult to see for yourself if mold/dust exists or if there has been an infestation of pests, which is why SameDay Services offers duct inspections. If you are concerned about your home's ducts leaking or needing to be cleaned, ask the HVAC expert.a Sameday A/C Services

Gas or Electric Boiler Repair and Replacement

Boilers heat homes by way of hot water or steam. At SameDay Services we can test, inspect or clean the boilers in your home. If your heating system does not have a high efficiency level, you may want to replace it.

Air Handler

The air handler is the principle air delivery system for your heating and air conditioning equipment. Comprised of various parts, we troubleshoot and diagnose issues that will keep the air moving within your home.
